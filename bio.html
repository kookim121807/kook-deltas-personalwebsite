About Me

At first glance, some people might assume I am a STEM-only nerd. That is not completely true.

I am not the biggest fan of biology, and I also enjoy philosophy, especially Greek philosophy. I also find macroeconomics surprisingly fun. One fun fact is that I once tried to learn Ancient Greek but did not stick with it. I would still love to learn it in the future.

Even so, my favorite subjects in school are mathematics, computer science, physics, and anything related to numbers, systems, and shapes. I am always open to learning new things, especially topics I have never explored before. Recently, I have also become interested in materials science and mechanical engineering.
