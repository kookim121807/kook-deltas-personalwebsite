A Hybrid Certificate Method for Upper Bounds on Sidon Sets

Applied Mathematics / Combinatorics / Discrete Mathematics

This project targets stronger upper bounds for the maximum size of a Sidon set by blending hand verification with computation. Prior work reached a bound near 0.998 through rigorous analysis, while computational approaches tightened results further. I am developing new theorems and lemmas to support a stronger, verifiable bound.
